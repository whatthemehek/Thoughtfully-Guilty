RECs, or Renewable Energy Credits, are a convenient and effective way of supporting clean energy usage. Basically, for a certain extra fee, your provider will add renewable energy onto the grid. This can either be done in blocks or based on your usage. If you buy a block of renewable energy, you essentially pay a fixed price for a fixed amount of kW*hrs of renewable energy added to the provider's grid.
